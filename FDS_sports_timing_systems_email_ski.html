Set-up in minutes (Video)

Photocells automatically connect over our robust radio protocol to the TBox timer for a quick and easy wireless set-up. Timing and scoring is then performed on our dedicated Smart Chrono iOS app via a Bluetooth connection to the timer. It’s that simple.
